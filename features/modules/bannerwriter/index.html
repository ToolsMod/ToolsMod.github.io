Banner writer

Requirements Description
Creative The gamemode must be creative
Build rights The user must be able to build in the world

How to use

Go into the gui of the bannerwriter (Right click on the Button) in the section Special

Write the text into the textfield and select your letter and background color.

When you have choosen the text and your colors, click on the Start button.

The bannerwriter will give you the first banner.

Now you can place the banners and the bannerwriter will automatically provide you with the next banner.

When the Text is finished, the bannerwriter will deactivate itself.
